Here's a time line of Lana del Rey's life:

  • 1985 - Born in Manhattan, NYC as Elizabeth Woolridge Grant
  • 2004 - Enrolls at Fordham University in The Bronx where she majored in Philosophy with emphasis on metaphysics
  • 2006 - First live performance for the Williamsburg Live Songwriting Competition
  • 2010 - Chooses "Lana del Rey" as her stage name
  • 2011 - Uploads self made music videos titled "Video games " and "Blue Jeans" to youtube. Becomes an instant global hit .
  • 2012 - Releases her first album "Born to die : The Paradise edition".
  • 2014-2016 - Releases albums : Ultraviolence and Honeymoon, becoming a globally recognized artist.
  • 2017-2019 - Releases Lust for Life and Norman F Rockwell albums, reaching the Top 20 on the Billboard Hot and was certified double-platinum in the US.
  • 2020-Present - Releases the album Chemtrails over the Country Club and her poetry collections
